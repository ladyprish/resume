The Philosophy
Behind the Work

Two career experiences shaped how I approach marketing: my background in innovation and being advised by Gartner GTM analysts.

Innovation wired me to be hypothesis-led. When you're building from scratch, the fastest path isn't louder marketing—it's smarter learning. I'm allergic to busywork and drawn to the work that clarifies what's true.

Gartner added the outside-in muscle: how markets form, how executives make decisions, and what makes a story "click" in 10 seconds. It taught me to treat positioning as a category decision, not a copy exercise—and to anchor proof where buyers actually look for it.

The throughline is people. The best strategy dies without a team that can execute it with urgency and rigor. I build environments where expectations are clear, ownership is real, and accountability is consistent—so the work compounds.

Innovation-Minded

Small bets, tight feedback loops, fast iteration. I turn learning into repeatable plays—not one-off campaigns.

Gartner-Trained

Category frameworks, buyer context, executive clarity. I turn market narrative into positioning that converts in pipeline.

People-Driven

High standards, direct feedback, real accountability. I give teams clarity in direction and the autonomy to execute.
